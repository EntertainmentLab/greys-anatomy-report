Handoff vs. Treatment: Show Recommendation Effects

Analysis Purpose:
This analysis examines how viewing social media content about a show influences people's enjoyment and willingness to recommend the show, focusing specifically on the comparison between the Handoff and Treatment groups only. The Handoff condition showed participants social media content about Grey's Anatomy after viewing the episode, while the Treatment condition showed only the episode content.
